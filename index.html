Beautician

Make any text look sexy

How it works

Drop your plain-text or Markdown text into Beautician, choose a theme, and Beautician will spit out a prettified version of your text that you can print or download as a PDF.

Beautician is perfect for resumes, proposals, or any writing that you want to present beautifully and professionally but don't want to mess around with formatting option in big, clunky word processors.
